Cookie Policy

Effective Date: June 1, 2025 · Last Updated: June 27, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how TrailsTV, LLC uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on the Lake Tahoe Planner at trailstv.com. It should be read alongside our Privacy Policy.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by a website when you visit. They allow the site to remember your actions and preferences over time. Cookies can be "session cookies" (deleted when you close your browser) or "persistent cookies" (remain on your device for a set period). Similar technologies include local storage and session storage — we use these for the same purposes described in this policy.

3. Essential Cookies

Essential cookies are strictly necessary for the Service to function. They enable core features like account sign-in and membership tier access. You cannot opt out of essential cookies while using the site.

4. Functional Cookies

Functional cookies remember your preferences to improve your experience. You may disable them in your browser settings, though some features may not work as expected.

5. Analytics Cookies

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the Lake Tahoe Planner. Data is aggregated and anonymized with IP anonymization enabled. You may opt out via the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

6. Third-Party Cookies

OAuth sign-in providers (Google, Apple, Facebook) may set their own cookies. Embedded YouTube videos on activity pages may set cookies from youtube.com if you interact with them.

7. Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Apple Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

Note that disabling cookies may prevent some features from working, including staying signed in and saving trip preferences.

8. Do Not Track

We honor DNT signals by not loading analytics cookies when DNT is enabled. Essential and functional cookies necessary for the Service to operate are unaffected.
